Monsters From the Sea

One of the key figures in 19th century paleontology was Mary Anning. Anning made many spectacular fossil discoveries in the seaside cliffs of Lyme Regis, England. Some of her notable finds were bones of prehistoric aquatic reptiles such as ichthyosaurs and long-necked plesiosaurs.
